Fake Money Clicker

By Fractyl (Fractylizer)

Released on 5/5/2024

The game is about gaining as much fake money as possible by making bills and selling goods.

Game Link - https://orteil.dashnet.org/igm/?g=7A1WPA9j

v0.1 Link - https://orteil.dashnet.org/igm/?g=FqNyniC3

Game Details

In Fake Money Clicker, you draw fake dollar bills which you use to hire others to draw them for you. You buy printers and factories to print fake money, trees to grow fake money, and laboratories to create fake money using science! Farms and mines produce goods, which you sell using shops to gain fake money. You can conduct research and buy different fake currencies and fake goods.

Fake Money Clicker began development on the 29th of March 2024. It was released on Fractyl's Discord on the 27th of April, then it was released on Fractyl's website and the Dashnet Forums Discord on the 5th of May. It was also featured in Episode 2 of IronDizaster's Playing Your Idle Game Maker Games series. The v0.1 update of the game released on the 6th of May and is playable via the v0.1 link. As of right now (25 June), Fake Money Clicker was last updated on the 22nd of June, with the v0.124 update.
